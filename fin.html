Finance and Billing

Payment Options

We offer multiple payment options for your convenience:

  • Online Payment: You can pay your bills online through our secure portal. Please have your patient ID ready when making online payments.
  • Offline Payment: You can visit our hospital and pay your bills at the billing counter. We accept cash, cheques, and major credit/debit cards.

Online Payment Details

To pay your bills online, please visit our website and navigate to the online payment section. You will need to enter your patient ID and follow the instructions to complete the payment.

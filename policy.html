Healthcare Policies

Financial Assistance Policy

We offer financial assistance programs for patients who are unable to afford their medical bills. Eligibility is based on financial need, and assistance may include discounted or free services.

Insurance Policy

We accept a wide range of insurance plans and work with patients to maximize their insurance benefits. Our staff can assist patients with understanding their insurance coverage and processing claims.

Price Transparency Policy

We are committed to providing transparent pricing information to our patients. Patients have the right to request and receive estimates of the cost of their medical services before receiving treatment.

Patient Rights and Responsibilities Policy

We uphold the rights of our patients to receive respectful and compassionate care. Patients also have responsibilities, including providing accurate information and following treatment plans.

Complaints and Grievances Policy

We have a process in place for addressing patient complaints and grievances. Patients are encouraged to provide feedback about their experiences, and we take all concerns seriously and work to resolve them promptly.
